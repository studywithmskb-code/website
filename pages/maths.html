Tips to Score Good Marks in Maths

1. Understand, Don't Memorize

Maths is about logic. Instead of memorizing steps, try to understand why a formula works. Once you get the concept, solving problems becomes a game!

2. Practice, Practice, Practice

Mathematics is a skill, like swimming or coding. The more you practice, the better you get. Solve at least 5 different types of problems daily.

3. Clear Your Doubts

Never let a doubt stay in your mind. If you are stuck, ask your teacher or watch the explanations again. A small doubt today can become a big gap tomorrow.

4. Maintain a Formula Book

Keep a small notebook just for formulas and key concepts. Review it before solving problems or going for an exam.
